Inkcareceration Music Festival


Inkcarceration is a Hard Rock music festival that is held in Mansfield OH.

In addition to the rock festival there will be more than 65 tattoo artists and haunted attractions at the historic grounds at OSR (Ohio state reformatory).


Headliners

  1. Limp Bizkit
  2. Pantera
  3. Slipnot
