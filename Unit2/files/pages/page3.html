Delux Detail

If you want your vehicle completely refreshed, top to bottom, front to back, inside and out, this is the professional detailing packages for you.


This package includes all services listed under our interior and exterior packages below. Lasting about 8 hours, this process is sure to leave you (and your vehicle) feeling rejuvenated.


  • Shampoo and hot water extract all carpets, floor mats, and seats
  • Remove dirt and stains from headliner
  • Detail all cracks, crevices, and panels
  • Wash windows, mirrors, and sunroof
  • Freshen and rejuvenate leather seats
  • Clean and wipe out glove box and center console
  • Vacuum spare tire compartment and trunk
  • Complete hand wash and rinse
  • clay bar treatment
  • Proper cleaning of wheels, wheel wells, and rims
  • Wipe down door jambs and inside fuel door
  • Treat tires, trim, and grill with premium exterior dressing
  • wax sealant
